How it works:
  • Drop your image. The painted mask tells the tool what to do with the dark pixels of your drawing. It never adds new marks outside your line art.
  • 🎯 Easiest workflow: hit Auto-detect first (marks everything pink/hidden by default), then use the blue brush to repaint over the parts you want visible (TAP ME text, bold elements, etc.).
  • You can also paint manually from scratch: pink over line art you want hidden, blue over bold elements that should stay visible.
  • Right-click or use the eraser to remove mask paint.
  • You don't need to paint precisely, just cover the region. The dither pattern only applies to dark pixels that are under your paint.
  • When you're done, hit Generate. Download and upload to Twitter via the app with HD uploads ON.
Pattern used: The exact 4×4 diagonal dither tile extracted from real tap-me artist uploads. Solid areas get the inverted pattern (62.5% black, looks near-solid). Hidden areas get the normal pattern (37.5% black, vanishes in thumbnails).
