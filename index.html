Three tools · one studio

Tap_STUDIO

Make images that hide in the X/Twitter feed and reveal themselves on tap. Pick the technique that fits your image — everything runs locally, nothing is uploaded.

Color Studio

Keeps the original colors. Paint what should hide, stay visible, or go solid black — with brush, bucket, magic wand, quick-select and smart lasso. Hidden areas become sparse interlaced pixels.

full color selection tools interlaced hiding

Brush Dither

Black-and-white, using the exact 4×4 dither tile from real tap-me uploads. Best for line art, sketches and drawings. Paint hidden, shadow, visible or pure-black regions by hand.

black & white 4×4 dither line art

Sheer Shade

Brush a see-through dark layer over skin so it reads like sheer tights or stockings — the skin's shape still shows through. Adjustable strength, tint and sheen.
